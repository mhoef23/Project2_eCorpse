What is the Exquisite Corpse?

Exquisite corpse, also known as exquisite cadaver (from the original French term cadavre exquis), is a method by which a story is collectively assembled. Each collaborator adds to a composition in sequence, by being allowed to see only the end of what the previous person contributed.

On this site, each story element is limited to 500 characters. The contributor is only given the last paragraph of the story as a prompt. While the entire living story can be viewed from the main page, the best option is write your contribution based only on the last element provided.
